Frequently asked

How do I play?

You're dealt teams and eras, then you draft an all-time roster from what you're given. The game runs a full season and the playoffs to produce your record. The goal is to go undefeated and build an all-time season, across both football and basketball.

Is it free?

Yes. All-Timers is free to play.

Do I need an account?

You can play, but an account lets you save progress, keep streaks, appear on leaderboards, and challenge friends across devices.

How do I delete my account and data?

You can delete your account directly inside the app — open your profile settings and choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated gameplay data. If you can't access the app, email support@aweindustries.com from your account email and we'll handle it.

Are the leaderboards fair?

Scores are verified on our servers, not trusted from the device, so the leaderboard reflects real results.

Is this affiliated with the NFL or NBA?

No. All-Timers is an independent game and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any professional sports league, team, or player. We reference player names and statistics as factual information only, and use no team names, logos, or likenesses.

Where to play

Play now on the web at alltimersgame.com. iOS and Android versions are on the way.
